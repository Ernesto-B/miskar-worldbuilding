## Overview
- Small, predominantly [[peoples/beastfolk]] settlement on the western shore of [[locations/iyresa/iyresan-bay]], thriving on fishing and simple crops.
- Peaceful respite for travelers, yet tied closely to the bay’s ancient beastfolk history.

## Description
- Rustic community harvesting the bay’s bountiful waters and tending sparse fields, with deep respect for nearby shrines.
- Resentment simmers toward outsiders who would claim the bay’s ruins or treasures.

## Inhabitants
- Tight-knit beastfolk families, fishers, and shrine caretakers; travelers and adventurers pass through for supplies and stories.

## Points of Interest
- **The Market Square:** Center of trade for seafood, seaweed, handmade goods, and minor enchanted fishing gear; known for spiced fish stew and fermented seaweed.
- **The Dockyard:** Wooden piers serving fishing boats and small traders, and a launch point for ventures deeper into the bay.
- **The Shrine Keeper’s Hut:** Home of the elderly caretaker guarding the island shrine and its lore.

## Daily Life
- Days revolve around tide schedules, fishing runs, and maintaining boats, with evenings spent sharing stories of the bay’s secrets.

## Factions & Influence
- Community elders and shrine keepers steer local decisions; visiting nobles or scholars from Cascadea and Stonefall draw suspicion.

## History
- Town identity shaped by the legacy of the sunken beastfolk city lying beneath the bay.

## Secrets & Rumors
> [!note]
> Some claim Cascadea’s wealth rests on relics stolen from the sunken city, a grievance that fuels local distrust of human expeditions.

## Adventure Hooks
- Seasonal fishing contests sometimes turn deadly when sea monsters or saboteurs strike mid-competition.
- Rivalry with Humans: A Cascadean or Stonefall noble sends a team to seize bay treasures; locals hire protection.
- Whispers link Cascadea’s rise to plundered artifacts from the sunken beastfolk stronghold.
