## Overview
- Southeastern sentinel of the [[regions/aetheris-isles/index|Aetheris Isles]], hardened by storms and the nearby [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides]].
- Rugged terrain and harsh seas make it a strategic frontier and training ground for disciplined warriors.

## Description
- Dense forests, rolling hills, and jagged coastline; temperate north with a wind-battered southern coast.
- Surrounded by the [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides]] and primarily accessed via [[locations/aetheris-isles/westreach-port]].

## Inhabitants
- People renowned for resilience, discipline, and martial training shaped by constant storms.

## Points of Interest
- [[locations/aetheris-isles/stormhold]]: Fortress city above the [[locations/aetheris-isles/underground-labyrinth]].
- [[The Three Pillars#The Sea Pillar|Sea Pillar]] Temple, the "Keeper of Depths."
- [[locations/aetheris-isles/gale-sanctum]]: Site of the Stormhold Martial Festival.
- South Port: Safe access point to and from [[locations/aetheris-isles/westreach-port]].

## Daily Life
- Life revolves around drills, seafaring, and weathering frequent storms; ports balance trade with vigilant defenses.

## Factions & Influence
- Devotion to the [[The Three Pillars#The Sea Pillar|Sea Pillar]] shapes law and tradition; martial orders recruit from festival champions.

## History
- The Labyrinth's Curse: The [[locations/aetheris-isles/underground-labyrinth]] was once a [[world/systems/floatstone]] mine, abandoned after a collapse many blame on the Sea Pillar, spawning the monsters now within.

## Secrets & Rumors
> [!note]
> The Black Tides Legend speaks of a ghostly ship appearing in the worst storms, its cursed crew roaming the [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides]] and pointing toward hidden treasures.
