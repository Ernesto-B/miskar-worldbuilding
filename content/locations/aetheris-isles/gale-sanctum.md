## Overview
- Cliff-carved arena on the western edge of [[locations/aetheris-isles/vorynth-island]] that hosts the Stormhold Martial Festival.
- Open design immerses combatants in the roar of the [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides]] and the island’s relentless winds.

## Description
- Massive statues of Vorynth’s greatest warriors and leaders ring the cliffs, each bearing symbolic weapons, shields, or floatstone orbs.
- Wind turbines and sails power moving platforms, gates, and banners, while floatstone platforms rise and shift mid-battle.
- Dynamic hazards—opening pits, floating obstacles, and gust-driven terrain—test agility alongside strength.
- The annual festival opens with a Tide Blessing performed by priests of the [[The Three Pillars#The Sea Pillar|Sea Pillar]].

## Inhabitants
- Priests who maintain the sanctity of the arena and warriors from across the Isles seeking renown.

## Points of Interest
- Main battleground of stone and floatstone platforms that reconfigure during contests.
- Cliffside statues serving as both guardians and memorials to Vorynth’s champions.

## Daily Life
- Outside festival season, the arena trains fighters in wind-swept drills and maintains the mechanisms that power its shifting platforms.

## Factions & Influence
- Sea Pillar clergy oversee rituals; victors often attract attention from the [[factions/aetheris-isles/ascendant-wardens|Ascendant Wardens]].

## History
- Annual festivals are rumored to grant the Sea Pillar’s favor to the champion, elevating them toward service with the Wardens.

## Secrets & Rumors
> [!note]
> Hidden chambers beneath the arena supposedly hold relics tied to the Sea Pillar, open only to those judged worthy.

## Adventure Hooks
- Trial by Combat: A party member accused of breaking Vorynth’s laws must win ritual combat at the Gale Sanctum to prove innocence.
