## Overview
- Ruling body of the [[regions/aetheris-isles/index|Aetheris Isles]], with each councilor representing [[locations/aetheris-isles/kaelyth-island|Kaelyth]], [[locations/aetheris-isles/vorynth-island|Vorynth]], or [[locations/aetheris-isles/dremscar-island|Dremscar]].
- Councilors are drawn from influential guilds, military leaders, or prominent families to oversee governance, resources, and defense.
- Convene in **Skystead** only for grave threats like invasions or disruptions to the [[world/systems/ascension-zone|Ascension Zone]]; otherwise rule from their home islands.
- Delegates manage day-to-day affairs in their absence.
- Political tension and power plays arise as island priorities clash, resolved through negotiation or shifting alliances.

## Ideology & Goals
- Preserve unity and security of the Isles while balancing each island’s needs.

## Structure & Hierarchy
- Three councilors with appointed delegates; decisions require collaboration across islands.

## Resources & Influence
- Authority over regional governance, resource allocation, and island defenses.

## Relations
- Mediates between island factions and liaises with guilds like the [[factions/aetheris-isles/ascendant-guild]].

## Operations
- Strategic sessions in Skystead for crises; routine governance handled locally on each island.

## Public Perception
- Viewed as necessary arbiters; locals debate favoritism toward their own island’s interests.
