## Overview
- Prestigious merchant guild headquartered in [[locations/iyresa/cascadea]], controlling trade routes across the [[Black Tides]] and hosting high-class auctions of rare artifacts, magical items, and exotic goods in the Velvet [[world/geography/drift]].

## Ideology & Goals
- Dominate luxury trade and secure exclusive access to exotic wares.

## Structure & Hierarchy
- Merchant council directs regional factors and auctioneers; trusted captains manage trade routes.

## Resources & Influence
- Wealth from rare-item auctions, fleets running Black Tides routes, and connections with elite buyers.

## Relations
- Courts wealthy patrons in Cascadea; competes with rival traders and navigates tensions with pirates on the Black Tides.

## Operations
- Organizes auctions, secures trade caravans and shipping contracts, and scouts for rare goods to corner markets.

## Public Perception
- Viewed as refined but cutthroat traders whose opulent auctions set regional prices.
