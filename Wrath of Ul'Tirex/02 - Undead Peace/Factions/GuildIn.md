# Summary
The international business Guild with a great reputation among all countries around the world and often will have authority over all business matters not effected by politics. Being a member is a testament to one's ability in their respective field as all members need to pass a difficult entrance exam that only happens once a year.

# Leadership
A governing body made up of Guild Masters from different Countries.

# Appearance
There is no set uniform for members, but all Members carry an [[Identification Crystal]] on them to prove their membership with GuildIn.

# Lore
- The whole Organization have less than 1000 members scattered around the world

## Membership
- Every year the annual GuildIn entrance exam is held
- Anyone is able to enter but only 10-20 people are accepted each year
- The exam consist of tests that change every year to evaluate the competent of each adventurer to see if they are worthy to become a GuildIn Member
