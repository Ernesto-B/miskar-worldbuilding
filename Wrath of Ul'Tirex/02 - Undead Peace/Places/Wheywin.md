# Location
- North of [[Meadheaven]]
- Lots of surrounding livestock

# Description
Surrounding the town is a number of livestock farms, more than the typical town would have. The town lacks any form of walls, instead a beaten path used for running surrounds the town with a few watch towers spaced around because, who is dumb enough to raid the HQ of the [[Church of the Gains]]? Inside the town reveals a rather simple town, no significant influence of technology or magic to be seen besides in the laboratories of the Church. There is a vast variety of races in Wheywin as the [[Church of the Gains]] is a very accepting of other races, even elves who are not well liked in [[Kingdom of Drakonia|Drakonia]]. There are various open gyms where members are seen working out and you can hear the hype of members cheering on each other to hit a new pr. Although the town is swarming muscular men and women, there are normal sized people here too, running the shops, trading and travelers coming through to take in the "Town of Muscle."
