# Mission
Attend [[Trenblo - Mateo]]'s Funeral

# Plot
The Party is invited to the main Church of the [[Church of the Gains]] to attend [[Trenblo - Mateo|Trenblo]]'s funeral as they were the last people to be around [[Trenblo - Mateo|Trenblo]] when he died. During the Party is tasked with helping various parts of the Ceremony and even meet Trenblo's mother, [[Trencille]]. 

# What to do
- [ ] Get to the [[Church of the Gains]] main Church located in [[Wheywin]] a town North of [[Meadheaven]]
- [ ] Help with the Ceremony (more info [[Church of the Gains#Burial/Funeral|here]])
	- [ ] Lift the Coffin (**DC23 Athletics**)
	- [ ] Start the Fire (**DC23 Acrobatics/Slight of Hand**) *I just wanted to have a dex check*
	- [ ] Collect the ashes (**DC23 CON save**)
		- Take 5 fire damage on a fail
	- [ ] Comfort [[Trencille]] during the ceremony
