# Lore
- A known gang but have never seen as a serious threat to the Kingdom of Drakonia if their Gang were to be found out
- The gang’s have seemingly “disappeared” about 2 years ago from the Kingdom of Drakonia
- [[Wrath of Ul'Tirex/01 - Bloodgold Revenge/Places/Galkoff|Galkoff]]
	- About 3 years ago the Blood Gold Gang came across Galkoff and seeing it at its current state, decided to take over the town.
	- Took about a year for the Gang to completely take over the town
	- Zhog keeps all the residence in order to not draw suspicion to themselves from the Drakonian Government
- A few months after taking over the Town of [[Wrath of Ul'Tirex/01 - Bloodgold Revenge/Places/Galkoff|Galkoff]], [[Zhog the Greedy|Zhog]] made a deal with the [[The Hand of the Sacred Flame]], Zhog started to fund the the cult in exchange to spare the gang and the townspeople.
	- The [[The Hand of the Sacred Flame]] came looking for a small town to eradicate and seeing the state of [[Wrath of Ul'Tirex/01 - Bloodgold Revenge/Places/Galkoff|Galkoff]], chose to invade as well. The [[Bloodgolds]] were quickly defeated overnight.
	- No one knows of this deal besides [[Zhog the Greedy|Zhog]], [[Shadow Runner]], [[Torug the Beast Tamer]] and [[Dealer the Gambler]]
