The party then runs errands, Oit scopes out the Barracks and talks to [[Lucas Stone]] who shows to be an ally within the corrupt guard within Galkoff. The party ends the day at the [[Wrath of Ul'Tirex/01 - Bloodgold Revenge/Places/Galkoff#Tavern - The Spider’s Pour|Spider's Pour]] where they killed more [[Bloodgolds]] that were trying to get rent money from [[Gorin]] for his tavern.
