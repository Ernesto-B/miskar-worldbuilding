# Description
The Protein Shake that the Knights from the [[Church of the Gains]] drink to optimize their diets and to strengthen them. The protein shake contains concentrated protein from many different protein dense creatures, the concentration is almost too much for any normal person to consume.

The High ranking members of the [[Church of the Gains]] become very busy with the responsibilities they take up, thus having a drink to cover for their protein goals when they miss meal while on missions.

# Effects
When consuming the Protein Shake, make a **CON** saving throw, **DC18**, or take **1d6 poison damage**.

After consuming the Shake, User gets advantage on all **Athletics** and **Acrobatics** checks for the next **hour**.
