# Traits
**Sky Flame Aura**: Nuts emits a faint aura of Sky Flames, giving it resistance to fire damage.

**Harmony Assimilation**: Once per short rest, as an action, Nuts can blend with its surroundings, becoming invisible until it moves or attacks, or until the end of its next turn.
