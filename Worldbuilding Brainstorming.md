# Magic

It could be a taboo they're forbidden from practicing, a consequence they suffer for using any kind of magic at all, or even the necessity of a tool. You can add more rules, of course, but having One Major Rule can be thematically impactful and, more importantly, it will be exciting when that rule is broken

- How do they find out they can do magic?
- How are they trained?
- What are the limits and boundaries of magic, what can it do, what can it never do, what is safe, what is dangerous
- What tools are required
- Magic and Healing
