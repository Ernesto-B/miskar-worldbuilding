# Weapons
## Greatsword
- Big Damage, shit move speed
- +8 attack, -5ft of movement
- **Damage**: 2d6 + 5

## Duel Daggers
- 2 attacks :)
- **Damage**: 1d4 per dagger

## Scythe
- AOE = W , shit single target
- Damage/on-hit scaling (+2 per enemy in range)

## Longbow
- Long range = Damage
- **Damage**: 1d6
- **Range**: 300ft
- +1 dmg for ever 30ft (5 tiles) creature is from user

## Handcannon
- Glass cannon
- **Damage**: 1d8
- **Range**: 30ft
- Lower Crit Chance to 18+
- Have to reload after one shot
	- No reload after 1 kill

## Parry Gloves
- Mix of Offence/Defense
- **Damage**: 1d4
- Resource Management
	- When opponents miss on the user, gain 1 parry points
	- Parry Points can be spent for either:
		- +3 to your next attack roll
		- +2 to your next damage roll

## Rope Dart
- Single target CC
- **Damage**: 1d4
- **Range**: 10ft
- On hit, you can move the creature you hit 5ft in any direction
	- Creatures Large and above require a **STR** check

## Stick of Whacking
- Its shit 
- **Damage**: *dmg mod* + 1 (basically an unarmed strike)
- Get creative with this :)
