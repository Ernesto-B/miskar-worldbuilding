The random Potions found in the hut in the [[Galkoff#Mines|mines]] 

There are a total of 5 bottles on the table

# Bottle with Blue Liquid
- Has the effects of [Potion of Climbing](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/wondrous-items:potion-of-climbing)

# Bottle with Swirling White Liquid
Has the effects of [Potion of Breath](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/wondrous-items:bottled-breath)

# Bottle with Glowing Yellow Liquid
- Has the effects of [Potion of Resistance](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/wondrous-items:potion-of-resistance)
	- Become resistant to **Piercing** damage for 1 hr

# Bottle with Fizzing Green Liquid
- Gives the user **Advantage** on all **Strength** checks for **1 hr**

# Small round bottle with pitch-black liquid
- A bottle of [Alchemist Fire](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/19-alchemists-fire-flask?srsltid=AfmBOooAlvqdwR0a_fOEzKthZFDqy66cWYqgfmYQCxFWAcIMYZJ49CsM)
