# Crew Archetypes
## A. The Loyal Old Salt
**Personality**
- Served under Vincent for years
- Protective, suspicious of outsiders

**What They Say**
- Defends the captain instinctively
- Mentions habits and routines
- Notices “small changes” but rationalizes them

**Good Clues**
- “Cap always tied that knot himself.”
- “He hasn’t been below deck much.”

**Bad Questions Trigger**
- Accusations → shuts down
- Insults → morale hit

## B. The Nervous New Hand
**Personality**
- Green, jumpy, sleep-deprived
- Afraid of being blamed

**What They Say**
- Heard noises
- Saw shapes at night
- Afraid to name names

**Good Clues**
- Scream timing
- Shadowy figures
- Inconsistent sightings

**Pressure Response**
- Cracks under gentle reassurance
- Lies under intimidation

## C. The Superstitious Sailor
**Personality**
- Believes the ship is cursed
- Reads omens everywhere

**What They Say**
- Talks about bad luck
- Mentions “wrong faces” or “ill winds”

**Good Clues**
- Describes doppleganger behavior metaphorically
- Mentions smells, chills, or “wrongness”

**Useful As**
- Foreshadowing, not facts


## D. The Practical Deckhand
**Personality**
- Focused on work
- Notices physical inconsistencies

**What They Say**
- Who was assigned where
- Who skipped tasks
- Changes in routine

**Good Clues**
- Delegated labor
- Missed watches
- Crate lifting inconsistency

## E. The Whisperer
**Personality**
- Knows gossip
- Talks to everyone

**What They Say**
- Contradictions between stories
- Who spoke to whom

**Good Clues**
- “That doesn’t line up…”
- Duplicate sightings
