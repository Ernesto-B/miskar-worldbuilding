# Last session's Recap
While party members were getting chase by the [[Bloodgolds]], [[Oitt - BK|Oit]] wakes up to find that all his shit has been looted off his body. After taking equipment from the Barrack's Armory, [[Oitt - BK|Oit]] spots [[Bibius - Elvind|Bibius]], [[Roronoa Zoro|Zoro]], [[Trenblo - Mateo|Trenblo]], [[Rite Lyft|Rite Lyft]] and [[Torrent - Kristen|Torrent]] being chased and joins them after getting spotted. The party loses them near the market district where they decide to loot [[Roland Whitehorn|Roland]]'s shop, [[Galkoff#Galkoff’s Pocket|Galkoff's Pocket]]. The party then tries to scope out the mines were they try to fight [[Big Yoshi|Yoshi]] who was guarding the mine's entrance but end up fleeing leaving behind [[Trenblo - Mateo|Trenblo]]. After licking their wounds, the party tries to sneak into the [[Galkoff#Tavern - The Spider’s Pour|Spider's Pour]] but is caught by [[Dealer the Gambler|Dealer]] who notices them and after planning for their capture, [[Oitt - BK|Oit]] uses the [[Gemstone of Brightness]] to blind everyone in the Tavern.
