# What do they do
- Their way of preaching is trying to convince people to go to the gym

## Missions
- Most missions are in the interest of the Church
	- Finding better, more efficient sources of protein
	- Studying new methods to train the body

# Lore Details
- Followers of the Church of the Gains are heavily dedicated to the art of body building. Keeping a consistent workout route, min/maxing meals to meet dietary goals.
	- Due to their dedication to body building, the Church does not discriminate towards anyone who is joining the church
- Body builders are treated as saints
- An magic users dedicate their studies to diet related topics such as finding the perfect protein potion for maximizing gains
	- To procure the protein needed to preform these experiments and to feed the order the Church of the Gains has a large amount of control and influence on the meat market in [[Kingdom of Drakonia]].
- Due to sheer dedication, all members of the Church of the Gains are unintentionally celibate.
- Due to their sheer strength, the [[Kingdom of Drakonia|Drakonian Army]] calls upon the Church when in times of need.
- The Church of the Gains has such a high need for protein that they control 1/3 of all meat production in the [[Kingdom of Drakonia]]. So much so that every other town has a Butchery owned by the church and is manned by the members.
	- Shops are called [[Holy Protein, Protein Shop]]

## Rituals
### Daily Protein
- Every day at from noon - 1pm all senior members must drink their [[Holy Protein Shake]] to maximize their gains.
- New or Junior Members need to put on a strict diet to be able to tolerate the condensed nutrients of the [[Holy Protein Shake]]

### Daily Prayer
- Prayers are preformed by lifting weights
- Members of the church workout as their form of prayer

### Burial/Funeral
- Deceased members are put into heavy coffins that need to be deadlifted and moved by 6 people to the Cremation Site
	- These 6 people do not need to be members of the Church but rather people who were close to the deceased and
- Some one must then use the ceremonial bow to start the fire for the Cremation
- The ashes are then collected with ceremonial gloves to be placed into an urn that looks like a protein container
